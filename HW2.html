Automaton Generations Exercise (v1.3)


Try out the process of generating successive cell states based on the rule shown.
The auto button will automatically fill in the active generation with the correct
answer. The check button will verify that all the cell values are correct and
will highlight any errors in red. Check will also advance to the next generation.

Clicking on a cell (in the active generation) will cause that cell to change
state. White cells are OFF. Black cells are ON. Cyan cells are in an unspecified
state.

Try auto-generating a generation of cell values then intentionally change a few
cell values by clicking on them. Run check and you should get some error
highlights.
